Things I do

Silksong

Slikong is a lesser-known indie action-platformer that blends precision movement with intense combat. The game challenges players with environments that require quick reflexes, creative problem-solving, and mastery of character mechanics. Unlike traditional platformers that focus mainly on jumping and avoiding obstacles, Silkong puts a strong emphasis on combining movement and fighting. Each encounter feels like a puzzle where you need to chain attacks, dodge enemy strikes, and use the terrain to your advantage.

The Binding of Issac

The Binding of Isaac is a roguelike dungeon crawler created by Edmund McMillen, one of the developers behind Super Meat Boy. The game follows Isaac, a young boy who flees into the basement of his home to escape his fanatically religious mother. What he finds is a twisted labyrinth full of monsters, strange power-ups, and nightmarish imagery drawn from biblical and psychological themes.

Biking

Biking, or cycling, is one of the most popular activities worldwide, enjoyed for recreation, transportation, and sport. At its simplest, biking provides a healthy and eco-friendly way to travel short or long distances. It reduces reliance on cars, cuts down on pollution, and offers a cost-effective alternative to fuel-powered transportation. Many cities have embraced biking by adding dedicated bike lanes, bike-sharing programs, and community riding events.
